My Life in the Pool

I started swimming when I was really young, and somewhere along the way it turned from just another activity into something I truly care about. Over the years, swimming has become more than just a sport; it's a routine, a challenge, and a part of who I am.

I currently swim for the Bellevue Club Swim Team in the Senior Prep group. Practices are tough, but they've taught me discipline and how to stay committed to long-term goals. I've grown a lot through the challenges, and it's made me mentally and physically stronger.

I've learned how to push through pain, focus under pressure, and keep going even when it's hard. Whether it's a long meet weekend or an early morning practice, I always walk away feel better than when I started.
